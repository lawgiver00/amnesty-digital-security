Digital Security for Amnesty International

Protecting Human Rights Work in the Age of Global Surveillance

1. What Should Amnesty Be Concerned About?

The NSA monitored internantional financial transactions, including SWIFT data, as the Snowden leaks exposed. This means the privacy and securtiy of activists, partners, and whistleblowers is potentially at risk. This is due to threats like metadata profiling, donor monitoring, or location analysis.

2. Our Recommendation: Implement FlowCrypt for End-to-End Encryption

FlowCrypt is a browser-based email encryption tool that integrates with Gmail while also supporting the OpenPGP standard. It allows Amnesty staff to send encrypted messages and attachments that only the intended recipients can read.

3. Why Amnesty Should Use FlowCrypt

FlowCrypt is easy to adopt, supports key verification, and empowers frontline workers in repressive environments. It addresses the very surveillance methods exposed by the NSA and helps Amnesty safeguard sensitive discussions and human rights reports.

4. How FlowCrypt Protects Amnesty’s Data

  • Encrypts email contents and attachments end-to-end
  • Prevents government agencies or internet providers from reading intercepted data
  • Allows verification of correspondent identities (via key matching)
  • Protects whistleblowers and partner activists from exposure

5. How to Use FlowCrypt

  1. Install the FlowCrypt browser extension
  2. Connect it to your Amnesty Gmail account
  3. Generate your public/private encryption keys
  4. Share your public key with trusted contacts
  5. Send encrypted messages using the "Secure Compose" button

6. Pros and Cons (from Defend Dissent)

  • ✔ Easy integration with existing email tools
  • ✔ Strong encryption using open standards (PGP)
  • ✘ Requires users to understand key sharing and verification
  • ✘ Not all users outside Amnesty may adopt the tool, limiting secure communication

Despite these limitations, FlowCrypt provides an essential layer of protection for an organization that depends on digital saftey.
